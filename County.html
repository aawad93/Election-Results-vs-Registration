However, election turnout is another factor that needs to be considered as well in this context. The graphs below show the difference between registration and results of each district by election turnout of the district. In other words, if the difference is 0, that means the results and registration of the district are identical. As you can see, the higher the turnout, the more the dots converge to 0 which would explain the outliers that were not included in our R squared values.
